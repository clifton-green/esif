Privacy policy

Any information in your application will be treated confidentially and will only be released if legally required, eg due to a Freedom of Information request.

If you think any sensitive information in your application shouldn't be disclosed then you must contact Department for Communities and Local Government (DCLG) or Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) explaining what information you think is sensitive and why.
